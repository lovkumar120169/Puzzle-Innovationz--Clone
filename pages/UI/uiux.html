What is user interface design? ▼
User interface design involves designing the visual elements, layout, and interactive features of a digital interface, like as a website or mobile application. It focuses on creating interfaces that are visually appealing, easy to navigate, and provide a seamless user experience.
Is this UI/UX bootcamp 100% online? ▼
Yes. All of Springboard’s courses are 100% online. That means you can learn in your space at your pace anytime, day or night. And just because you are learning online, doesn’t mean you are learning alone. With Springboard, you get a team committed to your success — one-on-one expert guidance from a mentor, a career coach to support you in landing the job you want, and a student advisor to help you navigate the ins and outs of the course. Plus, you’ll build connections with other ambitious students, just like you, who will be growing alongside and helping support you every step of the way.
What is user interface development? ▼
User interface development refers to the implementation and coding of user interface designs. It involves using programming languages and technologies to bring visual designs to life and create functional interfaces that users can interact with.
