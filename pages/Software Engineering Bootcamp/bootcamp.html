Is this data analytics bootcamp 100% online? ▼
Yes. All of Springboard’s courses are 100% online. That means you can learn in your space at your pace anytime, day or night. And just because you are learning online, doesn’t mean you are learning alone. With Springboard, you get a team committed to your success — one-on-one expert guidance from a mentor, a career coach to support you in landing the job you want, and a student advisor to help you navigate the ins-and-outs of the course. Plus, you’ll build connections with other ambitious students, just like you, who will be growing alongside and helping to support you every step of the way.
What are the prerequisites for admission? ▼
You should have strong critical thinking and problem-solving skills, and at least 2 years of professional experience working regularly with office, design or programming tools. If you don’t have this experience please do apply, as you have the option of the Foundations to Core program which will refresh your math skills and teach you data visualization and problem-solving frameworks in just 6-8 weeks before starting the bootcamp, at no extra cost. As part of the admissions process, we'll ask you to complete an online technical skills survey. Based on your results, we work with you to determine your ideal starting point.
How does the admission process work? ▼
Step 1: Submit your application Set aside 10-15 minutes to complete your application, which contains questions related to your background and professional goals. The application is free and we will process it immediately.

Step 2: Talk to admissions After you submit your application, our admissions team will follow-up with a phone call to review your application and answer any questions you have.

Step 3: Take the Technical Skills Survey Next, we’ll send a skills survey to figure out what you already know, so you’re set up for success from the very beginning. If you’re new to the field, don’t worry — we’ve got a plan for everyone.

Step 4: Reserve your spot When you pass the survey, we will send you a registration link. Choose the start date and payment plan that works for you! Then get ready to transform your career. Please note that spots in Springboard bootcamps are limited and we accept candidates on a rolling basis.
